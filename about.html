About Me

I have spent the last 20 years or so working in the tech industry in a range of roles including software analyst, network engineer and technical consultant.

I have always enjoyed coding and decided that 2023 was to be the year I would learn more about an area that has always interested me - web development. Luckily I was accepted on Netmatter's 16 Week skills bootcamp which covers a number of subject areas including HTML / CSS, Javascript, PHP and C#. It has been a great learning experience, albeit very intensive and I have expanded my knowledge greatly.

In my spare time I enjoy hiking (I like to try and spend at least one week per year in the mountains of Wales), cycling (yes, I am a MAMIL!) and playing video games (badly). I also enjoy deep dives into the inner intricacies of Javascript, particularly articles and books by Dr. Axel Rauschmayer.
